FAQ

  • Accommodation

    The wedding party will be staying at the Royal Chulan Hotel in Kuala Lumpur, and we recommend our guests to do the same for easier logistics around transportation and event updates. If you prefer a larger living space, we recommend using the AirBnB service to rent an apartment or townhouse. Please ensure that you stay close to the wedding venue(s), for shorter commutes times.

  • Transportation

    We will provide bus services to the wedding venue on the day. For your personal trips around Kuala Lumpur, please download a rideshare app like Grab. We recommend downloading and setting up this app before arriving in Malaysia, so that you are ready to go as soon as you land.

  • I have dietary requirements. Who do I contact?

    Please use the contact form below, or send us an email at: shaneeljessy2026@gmail.com

  • Currency Conversion

    There are a few options for converting your currency to Malaysian Ringgits.

    1. There are currency conversion booths at the airport, and we recommend coverting some currency to cash in case of emergencies.
    2. Download and sign up for the Wise Card a few months ahead of time. This app lets you quickly convert between currencies, and provides a physical card that you can use in place of credi/debit cards. The currency conversion is done using the app, and usually takes less than 10 minutes.
    3. Inform your bank that you will be travelling overseas so that transactions on your Via or Mastercard will be permitted. This is a necessary step an your bank may block your card for transactions occurring in another country.
